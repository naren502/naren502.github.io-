HOME & PERSONAL CARE

Sri Lanka population is around 21 million in 2012 and much more sophisticated ( in terms of quality) than any other country in South Asia being exposed to imported Personal Care products. Ranging from natural beauty care products to large scale Home Care industry manufacturing,  its requirements are adapting  to that of foreign markets.

 

A catalogue of cosmetic grade materials from our many reputed suppliers with robust techniques for the innovation of both: personal care and home care items, as new trends are adopted to service increasing buyer awareness - Piyoma with a decade of experience caters to broaden the vision of manufacturers with practical solutions.

Performance chemicals are a sub-set of a diverse range of speciality chemicals with industry specific requirements- coupled with application specific formulations and comfortable lead times for necessary projections of  volume, our professional courtesy extends to our Partners for higher return on investment .
